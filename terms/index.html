1. About Vybridge

Vybridge ("we", "us", "the platform") is a global marketplace that connects businesses who want to advertise with website owners and social media creators who have an audience to sell access to. We provide the technology that lists ad placements, collects payment, holds funds in escrow, and releases payouts — we are not a party to the underlying advertising arrangement between an advertiser and a publisher or blogger, and we do not create, endorse, or vouch for any advertisement placed through the platform.

2. Account roles

Every account on Vybridge has exactly one of the following roles:

  • Advertiser — books ad slots on publisher websites or sends paid content offers to bloggers, and pays for the placement.
  • Publisher — lists ad slots on their own website, approves or rejects submitted creatives, and gets paid when a placement completes.
  • Blogger — lists their Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube channel, accepts or declines paid content offers from advertisers, and gets paid when a published post is confirmed.

You are responsible for the accuracy of the information on your account and for all activity that happens under it.

3. Acceptable use

By using Vybridge you agree to:

  • Provide accurate account, site, and payment information.
  • Only submit ad slots, channels, and creatives that you have the legal right to offer or publish.
  • Not attempt to circumvent the platform's escrow, fee, or payout mechanisms.
  • Not use the platform to harass, defraud, or mislead any other user.

4. Escrow

When an advertiser books a slot or sends a blogger offer, the full amount — the placement price plus the platform fee — is charged immediately and held in escrow by our payment processor. Funds are not released to the publisher or blogger until the placement is approved and completed (or automatically confirmed, see Section 6). This protects both sides: the advertiser doesn't pay for a placement that never runs, and the publisher or blogger doesn't produce work without payment already secured.

5. Platform fee

Vybridge charges a platform fee on top of every booking or offer, shown to the advertiser before payment. New accounts start at a 12% fee. As an advertiser's total completed spend on the platform grows, their fee rate automatically decreases to a lower tier — there is nothing to negotiate or request; the applicable rate is calculated and shown at checkout every time.

6. Automatic confirmation

Once a creative is submitted or a blogger publishes a post, the counterparty is expected to review it promptly. If an advertiser does not approve or reject a published post within 48 hours, it is automatically confirmed as accepted and proceeds toward payout. This keeps the platform fully automated and prevents a placement from being held up indefinitely by inaction.

7. Prohibited content

The following may never be submitted as an ad creative, slot description, or blogger campaign, and will be rejected and may result in account suspension:

  • Content that is illegal in the advertiser's, publisher's, or blogger's jurisdiction.
  • Content that is deliberately misleading, deceptive, or makes false claims.
  • Adult or sexually explicit content that is not clearly and accurately labeled as such.
  • Malware, phishing pages, or anything designed to compromise a visitor's device or accounts.

8. Refunds

Because funds are held in escrow rather than paid out immediately, most disputes are resolved automatically by the deal's own status: a refund is issued to the advertiser only when a publisher rejects a submitted creative, or when a blogger declines an offer. Once a placement has been approved and has gone live, or a blogger's post has been published and confirmed (including by automatic confirmation), the placement is considered complete and is not refundable.
