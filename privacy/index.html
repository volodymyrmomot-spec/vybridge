Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

1. Data we collect

To create and run an account on Vybridge, we collect:

  • Your name and email address, used for login and account communication.
  • Your country, used to show relevant currency, tax, and audience information.
  • Payment information needed to charge or pay you — handled entirely by Stripe (see Section 2).
  • Content you submit yourself: site domains, ad slot details, blogger channel links, and uploaded creatives.

2. Payment data & Stripe

All payment processing — charging advertisers, holding escrow, and paying out publishers and bloggers — is handled by Stripe. We never receive or store your full card number, CVC, or bank account details; Stripe stores that data on its own PCI-compliant infrastructure and only shares limited information back to us (such as the last four digits of a card, or whether a payout succeeded).

Payments powered by Stripe

For details on how Stripe itself handles your data, see Stripe's Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

Vybridge uses a single, session-only cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or any cross-site behavioral tracking — the platform doesn't need to profile you to function, so it doesn't.

4. We don't sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties, for advertising or any other purpose. Your information is used only to operate the marketplace: matching advertisers with publishers and bloggers, processing payments, and communicating with you about your own deals.

5. Cloudinary (creative storage)

Ad creative images you upload — for a slot booking or a blogger offer — are stored using Cloudinary, a third-party media storage provider. Cloudinary hosts and serves these files on our behalf; it does not have access to your account, payment, or contact information.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account profile page, provided you have no active deals in progress. For any other data request, contact us at the email below.

7. Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile, site, slot, and channel data is removed immediately; records of completed financial transactions may be retained separately for as long as required by tax and accounting law, independent of your account's existence.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or a request regarding your personal data can be sent to privacy@vybridge.com.
