How It Works

From setup to your first payout

A simple process for website owners, blogs, and media — without hunting for advertisers manually.

Step 1

Add your platform

Register your website, blog, or media outlet and list your available ad placements.

Step 2

Receive requests

Advertisers discover your platform and send placement requests for your review.

Step 3

Approve ads

Review each request and accept only the campaigns that fit your audience.

Step 4

Earn from placements

After the ad goes live, get paid directly through the Vybridge platform.

What You Control

Full control over monetization

You set the terms — Vybridge simply connects you with advertisers.

Placement pricing

Set your own rates for each ad slot on your platform.

Ad formats

Choose which ad types you accept: banners, native posts, newsletters, and more.

Available slots

Specify the exact zones on your site or in your content where ads can appear.

Accepting or rejecting requests

No ad goes live without your approval — you always have the final say.
